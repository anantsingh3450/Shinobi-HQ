# Hokage Alpha Failure Criteria
## Phase 5.0 — Risk Violations & Emergency Shutdown Protocols

This document defines the conditions that constitute a failure of the Alpha Program, triggering immediate pause, termination, or system-wide emergency shutdown.

---

## 1. Hard-Stop Drawdown Violations
*   **Total Drawdown Breach:** Peak-to-trough equity decay exceeding $8.0\%$ (loss of ₹40,000.00 from starting capital).
    *   *Action:* `CapitalPreservationEngine` transition to `NO_TRADE` mode. All automated scan loops are disabled. Open positions are closed using market exit orders.
*   **Consecutive Losses Limit:** A streak of $\ge 5$ consecutive losing trades on a single strategy setup.
    *   *Action:* Puts that strategy in a 48-hour quarantine. Multipliers scale down to $0.2$, requiring a manual review of matching analog market conditions before reinstatement.

---

## 2. Compliance and Manual Intervention Violations
*   **Discretionary Order Cancellation:** Manual deletion or adjustment of orders generated by `ExecutionBot` on the venue.
    *   *Action:* Immediate emergency shutdown. The connection is disabled, and the bot triggers a `NO_TRADE` block.
*   **Stop-Loss Modification:** Discretionary widening or deletion of stop-loss levels for active positions.
    *   *Action:* Immediate emergency shutdown. System closes the position at market and enters a lockdown state.
*   **Veto Override:** Discretionary bypass of No-Trade decisions, sizer overrides, or risk failures in the Investment Committee.
    *   *Action:* Program terminated; Go status revoked.

---

## 3. Slippage and Execution Drag Shocks
*   **Average Slippage Variance:** Average slippage exceeding $2.0\%$ of the Average True Range (ATR) over any rolling window of 10 trades.
    *   *Action:* Pauses execution. Commits `SlippageAudit` to Layer 2 cache. Sizing sizers adjust execution limits to match wider spreads before resuming.

---

## 4. Preservation Violations
*   **Speculative Mingling:** Any transfer of capital between the core investment account and speculative allocations that exceeds the $10\%$ cap.
    *   *Action:* Triggers Capital Preservation violation, pausing all operations until accounts are re-aligned.
